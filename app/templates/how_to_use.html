How To Use

  1. Customers register by selecting their service type (Banks, Cafes, Clinics, or Tech Support). It is as simple as choosing your preferred option and getting closer to being served promptly.
  2. Upon registration, customers receive a ticket number along with an estimated wait time. This allows you to relax, browse, or attend to other matters without the risk of losing your place in the queue.
  3. Administrators log in to manage the queue by calling, skipping, or resetting tickets with ease, effectively overseeing the flow like skilled professionals.
  4. Subtle sound notifications alert administrators whenever actions occur, enhancing awareness and maintaining an engaging environment.
  5. Customers wait for their number to be called. Late arrivals are automatically skipped to ensure the queue progresses smoothly and fairly for everyone involved.

Pro Tip: Keep your phone within reach and be ready since your turn will arrive sooner than you expect.
