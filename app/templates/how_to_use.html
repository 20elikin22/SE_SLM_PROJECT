How To Use 🎯🎯🎯

  1. Customers register by selecting their service type 🏦☕🏥🖥️. It’s as easy as picking your favorite flavor — except this one gets you served faster!
  2. After registering, customers receive a ticket number 🎫🎟️🎫 and estimated wait time ⏳⌛⏰. Now you can chill, browse, or pretend you’re super busy without losing your spot.
  3. Admins login 🔐🛡️🗝️ to manage the queue: call, skip, or reset tickets with a click — like a queue ninja at work 🥷🥷🥷.
  4. Sound effects 🔔🎶🎵 notify admins when actions happen, adding a little pizzazz and keeping things lively (because boring queues are so last century).
  5. Customers wait for their number to be called 📣📢📣, and latecomers get skipped automatically — so nobody keeps everyone else waiting (sorry, not sorry!).

Pro Tip: Keep your phone handy, and maybe practice your victory dance — because your turn will come faster than you think! 🕺💃📱
